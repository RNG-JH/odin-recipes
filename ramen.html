Miso Ramen Recipe

Description

An easy to cook Miso Ramen for busy individuals

Flavoured with fresh miso, this easy to cook ramen can be completed in less than 30 MINUTES!

Ingredients are readily found in major convenience stores and supermarkets

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic
  2. In a medium pot, heat the sesame oil over medium-low heat and add the minced shallot
  3. Add the meat and increase heat until meat no longer pink
  4. Add sake and chicken stock and simmer
  5. Cook noodles well and add in soup
